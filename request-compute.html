Request a VM via the market

Not yet working — bidder discovery needs a CORS-enabled market relay. The relay connection, keypair generation, record creation, and policy mode selection all work. Track progress on GitHub →

Configure your VM, select a policy mode to control who can bid, and submit. The relay connects you to bidders on the decentralized market — you get a terminal and a receipt back.

Policy modes control who can bid on your RFP. only-me = just your own DIDs. tangled-vouch = anyone you have vouched for on Tangled's graph. mutuals = mutual follows on Bluesky. dynamic = delegate to a remote policy engine. Learn more →

Prefer the CLI?

The request-vm-ssh CLI does the same flow from your terminal — with full SSH, firehose bid watching, and all four policy modes.
