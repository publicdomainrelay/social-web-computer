For organizations

Hand your spare capacity to your own people

If your org already owns infrastructure — a Docker host, a DigitalOcean account — it can act as a bidder with pluggable compute providers for its own members — no shared root passwords, no internal ticket queue. A member's RFP is just another signed record, and your bidder decides whose it will even look at.
